COURSES

Undergraduate Courses:

GCET Jammu offere Bachelor of Engineering(B.Tech) degrees in the following disciplines:

  • 1.Computer Engineering

  • 2.Mechanincal Engineering

  • 3.Civil Engineering

  • 4.Electronics and Communication Engineering

  • 5.Electrical Engineering

Post Graduate Courses:

GCET Jammu offers M.Tech programs in:

  • 1.Mechanincal Engineering

  • 2.Computer Engineering

  • 3.Electronics and Communication Engineering

Candidates must have a B.E/B.tech dgree with atleast 60% marks and a valid GATE Score

ADMISSION

Admissions are based on the Common Entrance Test (JKCET) conducted by the Jammu and Kashmir Board of Professional Entrance Examinations (BOPEE). Candidates must have completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English, securing at least 50% marks (40% for SC/ST categories).
